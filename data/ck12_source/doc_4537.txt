The human heart.
An absolutely remarkable organ.
Obviously, its main function is to pump blood throughout the body.
And it does this extremely well.
On average, this muscular organ will beat about 100,000 times in one day and about 35 million times in a year.
During an average lifetime, the human heart will beat more than 2.5 billion times.
