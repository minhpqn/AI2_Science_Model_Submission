Q :
What is your frame of reference if you are standing on the sidewalk and see the bus go by?
How can you tell that the bus is moving?
A :
Your frame of reference might be the trees and other stationary objects across the street.
As the bus goes by, it momentarily blocks your view of these objects, and this helps you detect the busâ€™ motion.
Motion is defined as a change of position.
How we perceive motion depends on our frame of reference.
Frame of reference refers to something that is not moving with respect to an observer that can be used to detect motion.
