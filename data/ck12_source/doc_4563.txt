Specifically, our energy comes from what?
The respiratory and circulatory systems work together to provide cells with the oxygen they need for cellular respiration.
Cells also need glucose for cellular respiration.
Glucose is a simple sugar that comes from the food we eat.
To get glucose from food, digestion must occur.
This process is carried out by the digestive system.
