Making an electrical connection to the earth which results in all charge being removed.
capacitance:
The ability of an object to hold an electrical charge.
capacitor:
A device specifically designed to hold an electrical charge.
farad:
The unit of capacitance in the SI system equal to the capacitance of a capacitor having an equal and opposite charge of 1 coulomb on each plate and a potential difference of 1 volt between the plates.
