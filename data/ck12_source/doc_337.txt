A simple canker sore reemerges about three to four times every year, and is the common type in people between the ages of 10 and 20.
Canker sores are not contagious and usually heal on their own within a week or two.
Causes of canker sores include a viral infection, stress, hormonal fluctuations, food allergies, immune system problems, or mouth injuries.
