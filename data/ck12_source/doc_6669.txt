Units and Problem Solving
The Big Idea
Units identify what a specific number refers to.
For instance, the number 42 can be used to represent 42 miles, 42 pounds, or 42 elephants!
Numbers are mathematical objects, but units give them physical meaning.
Keeping track of units can help you avoid mistakes when you work out problems.
Describe the metric system and convert between metric units.
Students will learn about the metric system and how to convert between metric units.
