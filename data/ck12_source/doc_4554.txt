The trachea divides as it enters the lungs to form the right and left bronchi , which branch into smaller bronchioles within each lung.
The bronchioles lead to alveoli, the sites of gas exchange.
The organs of the respiratory system include the lungs, pharynx, larynx, trachea, and bronchi.
