The Significance of Carbon
A compound found mainly in living things is known as an organic compound .
Organic compounds make up the cells and other structures of organisms and carry out life processes.
Carbon is the main element in organic compounds, so carbon is essential to life on Earth.
Without carbon, life as we know it could not exist.
A compound is a substance that consists of two or more elements.
A compound has a unique composition that is always the same.
The smallest particle of a compound is called a molecule.
