The primary cause of tides is the gravitational attraction of the Moon.
This causes two high and two low tides a day.
When the Sun's and Moon's tides match, there are spring tides.
When the two tides are opposed, there are neap tides.
The difference between the daily high and the daily low is the tidal range.
