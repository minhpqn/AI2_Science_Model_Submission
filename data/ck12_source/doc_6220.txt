Why are seeds important?
Many important crops, such as corn, are planted and harvested as seeds.
These seeds are important sources of food.
For example, corn is ground into feed for chickens and cows.
And corn syrup is used to sweeten beverages and candy.
But most importantly, seeds are the starting point for a new plant.
