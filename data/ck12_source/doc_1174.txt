What is a Mineral?
A mineral is a solid material that forms by a natural process.
A mineral can be made of an element or a compound.
It has a specific chemical composition.
Its chemical composition is different from other minerals.
Each type of mineral has physical properties that differ from others.
These properties include crystal structure, hardness, density, and color.
For example, silver is a soft, shiny metal.
Salt is a white, cube-shaped crystal.
Diamond is an extremely hard, translucent crystal.
