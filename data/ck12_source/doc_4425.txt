But what is so different about opossums is that they are the only marsupial in North America.
These opossums, however, can be beneficial to humans.
They use their sharp teeth to crush bone – which means that they are good getting rid of unwanted rodents in your neighborhood.
They have excellent immune systems and they emit that terrible odor for protection.

Marsupials give birth to a tiny, immature embryo.
The embryo then continues to grow and develop in a pouch on the mother’s belly.
Marsupial development is less risky for the mother.
However, the embryo is fragile, so it may be less likely to survive than the fetus of a placental mammal.
