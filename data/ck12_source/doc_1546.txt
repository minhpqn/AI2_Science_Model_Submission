The most energetic UV light is harmful to life.
The atmosphere absorbs most of this UV light from the Sun.
Gamma rays have the highest energy, and they are the most damaging rays.
Fortunately, gamma rays don’t penetrate Earth’s atmosphere.
Ultraviolet radiation has the highest energy; infrared the lowest.
Solar energy travels in photons.
Photons travel in waves, which make up electromagnetic radiation.
Visible light is the wavelengths of light humans can see.
