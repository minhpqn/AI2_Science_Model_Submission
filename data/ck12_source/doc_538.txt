Diseases of the Immune System
The immune system usually protects you from pathogens and other causes of disease.
When the immune system is working properly, it keeps you from getting sick.
But the immune system is like any other system of the body.
It can break down or develop diseases.
AIDS is an infectious disease of the immune system caused by a virus.
Some diseases of the immune system are noninfectious.
They include autoimmune diseases and allergies.
