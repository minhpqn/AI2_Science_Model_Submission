center of gravity:
The point at which the entire weight of a body may be considered to be concentrated so that if supported at this point the body would remain in equilibrium in any position.
normal force:
In mechanics, it is the component, perpendicular to the surface of contact, of the contact force exerted on an object by, for example, the surface of a floor or wall, preventing the object from penetrating the surface.
