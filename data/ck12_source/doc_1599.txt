Are bacteria cells like our cells?
Yes and no.
Bacteria cells are similar to our cells in some ways.
Like our cells, bacteria cells have DNA and a plasma membrane.
But bacteria are unique in other ways.
They are called prokaryotic cells because of these differences.
Prokaryotic and Eukaryotic
There are two basic types of cells, prokaryotic cells and eukaryotic cells .
The main difference between eukaryotic and prokaryotic cells is that eukaryotic cells have a nucleus .
The nucleus is where cells store their DNA , which is the genetic material.
The nucleus is surrounded by a membrane.
