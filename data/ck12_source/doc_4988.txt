energy :
Property of matter that is defined as the ability to do work.
kinetic energy :
Form of energy that an object has when it is moving.
law of conservation of energy :
Law of physics that states that energy may neither be created nor destroyed; the sum of all the energies in a system remains constant over time.
matter :
All the substances of which things are made.
potential energy :
Form of energy that is stored in an object due to its position.
Energy is a property of matter.
It cannot be created or destroyed.
Organisms obtain light energy from sunlight or chemical energy from food and change the energy into different forms, including heat energy.
