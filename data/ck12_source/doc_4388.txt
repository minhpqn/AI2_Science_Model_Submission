Structure and Function in Birds
Birds are endothermic tetrapod vertebrates.
They are bipedal, which means they walk on two legs.
Birds also lay amniotic eggs with hard, calcium carbonate shells.
Although birds are the most recent class of vertebrates to evolve, they are now the most numerous vertebrates on Earth.
