Disorders of the Nervous System
There are several different types of problems that can affect the nervous system.
Vascular disorders involve problems with blood flow.
For example, a stroke occurs when a blood clot blocks blood flow to part of the brain.
Brain cells die quickly if their oxygen supply is cut off.
This may cause paralysis and loss of other normal functions, depending on the part of the brain that is damaged.
