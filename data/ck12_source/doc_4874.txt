prokaryotic cells :
Typical of simple, single-celled organisms, such as bacteria; lack a nucleus and other membrane bound organelles.
There are only two main types of cells: prokaryotic and eukaryotic.
Prokaryotic cells lack a nucleus and other membrane-bound organelles.
Eukaryotic cells have a nucleus and other membrane-bound organelles.
This allows these cells to have complex functions.
