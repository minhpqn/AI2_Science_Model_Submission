What are the organs that help you breathe?
When you think of the processes of breathing, the lungs probably come to mind.
The lungs are the main organ of the respiratory system.
However, many other organs are also needed for the process of respiration to take place.

The nose and the nasal cavity filter, warm, and moisten the air you breathe.
The nose hairs and the mucus produced by the cells in the nose catch particles in the air and keep them from entering the lungs.
Behind the nasal cavity, air passes through the pharynx , a long tube.
Both food and air pass through the pharynx.
