Despite the great loss of life, there was light at the end of the tunnel.
The Permian extinction paved the way for another burst of new life at the start of the following Mesozoic Era.
This included the evolution of the dinosaurs.
The Paleozoic Era began with the Cambrian explosion.
It ended with the Permian extinction.
During the era, invertebrate animals diversified in the oceans.
Plants, amphibians, and reptiles also moved to the land.
