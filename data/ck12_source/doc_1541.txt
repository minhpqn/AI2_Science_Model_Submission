What Is Energy?
What explains all of these events?
The answer can be summed up in one word: energy.
Energy is defined as the ability to do work.
Doing anything takes energy.
A campfire obviously has energy.
You can feel its heat and see its light.
You can use its energy to cook food.
Forms of Energy
Heat and light from the campfire are forms of energy.
