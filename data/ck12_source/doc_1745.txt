Deserts receive very little rainfall.
They are often home to very unusual and well adapted plants.
Steppes receive more rain than deserts.
They are higher and have grasses and scrub.
Plants adapt to local conditions.
If conditions are the same but the plants are far apart, they may still be very similar.
